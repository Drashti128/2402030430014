VR Lab Set Up Introduction:

A VR lab setup refers to a dedicated space equipped with the necessary hardware and software to allow users to experience immersive virtual reality environments. It typically involves high-powered computers, head-mounted displays (HMDs), motion tracking systems, and specialized software for research, training, education, or entertainment.

Key highlights of the VR lab environment:

  • Realistic Science Simulations
  • Interactive Experiments
  • Accessible from Anywhere
